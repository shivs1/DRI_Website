Who We Are:

The Team at Democratic Reforms Initiative is composed of young professionals from diverse backgrounds who are committed to working towards building a better future for the nation through a more paricipative and politically engaged next generation. We aim to bring to the forefront the good work done by politicains across the country while also creating a platform where the youth can contribute to nationbuilding by engaging with their political representatives.

What We Do:

Highlighting the Good

In a world where TV news has dedicated it's efforts towards highlighting how fast our world is going to end, and how corrupt our politicians are, we believe there needs to be a force which highlights the good done by many of our representatives. The DRI team analyses the impacts of interventions made by politicians and highlights it using the power of conventional and social media. This not only helps politicians gain recognition for their work, but also inspires people to join the efforts, leading to better interventions in the future.

Creating Good

The team at DRI actively works to bring together great ideas and those that can make it happen. We design campaigns and events where the the active engagement of political representatives and their constituents, especially the youth, lead to the creation of something everyone can be proud of. We manage everything from idea to execution and beyond. The belief that lasting change can only happen with the engagement of every citizen inspires us to bring together citizens and their political representatives for the common good.

Solving Problems

At the heart of every political representative lies his constituency. Every politician takes pride in knowing the pulse of his constituency, but sometimes a second reading helps. The DRI team helps politicians engage with their constituents and manage everything from finding the biggest problems in the constituency to working towards fixing things through innovative solutions. We engage with every level to ensure that the citizenry, administration and politicians work together to make the nation stronger.

  • What We'll Do For You:

    Brand Building and Social Media Management - Which including the creation of videos, infographics and articles, which we then disseminate to the widest audience possible.

    Event Management and Design of Campaigns - From analysis of what kinds of events would work best in a context through focus groups and research to the actual execution and documentation of the event, we take care of everything.

    Constituency Connect - We help bridge the gap between political representatives and their constituents by providing unique platforms for the sharing of ideas and concerns, which help us determine local issues of greatest concern. We then work on devising innovative solutions and implementing them with the support of all stakeholders.

  • How We Do It:

    The team at DRI has extensive experience in managing social media profiles of public figures and knows how to get the message out. We bank on extensive research and make full use of analytics and state of the art strategies to deliver results.

    We have successfully managed many campaigns and events in the past and know exactly what it takes to create an engaging and memorable event with the maximum coverage and output. Our team works with industry experts and grassroots organizations to ensure the successful execution of any event.

    All members of the DRI team have experience of working in the offices of Members of Parliament across party lines which gives us a qnique understanding of what public representatives and the citizens in their constituency need. This has helped us manage constituencies in a professional and systematic manner to ensure the best resolution of all public grievances.
